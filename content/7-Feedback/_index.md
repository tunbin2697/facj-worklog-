### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is friendly, open, and supportive. FCAJ members are always willing to help whenever I face difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is also neat and comfortable, which helps me stay focused. If possible, adding more informal gatherings or team bonding activities would make the team even closer.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor gives clear and detailed guidance, explains patiently when I do not understand, and always encourages me to ask questions. The admin team also supports administrative tasks, provides the necessary documents, and creates good conditions for me to work effectively. I especially appreciate that the mentor allows me to try solving problems on my own instead of giving the answer immediately.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks I was assigned were closely related to what I studied at university, while also introducing me to new areas I had not experienced before. This helped me strengthen my foundational knowledge and gain more practical skills at the same time.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned several new skills such as using project management tools, working in a team, and communicating professionally in a corporate environment. The mentor also shared practical experiences that helped me better understand my career direction.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other and works seriously while still keeping the atmosphere comfortable. When urgent tasks come up, everyone supports one another regardless of position. This made me feel like a real part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides an internship allowance and supports flexible working hours when needed. In addition, the chance to join internal training sessions is a valuable benefit.
