### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The environment is welcoming, professional, and highly collaborative. I always felt comfortable asking for help, and teammates consistently supported me when challenges came up. The working setup also helped me stay focused and productive. Going forward, a few more casual team activities would make collaboration even stronger.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
Mentor support was one of the strongest parts of this internship. Guidance was clear, practical, and patient, and I was encouraged to think independently before receiving hints. The admin team was also responsive and well-organized, which made the workflow smooth and efficient.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assignments aligned well with my academic background while introducing real-world cloud practices that I had not fully explored at university. This balance helped me connect theory with implementation and significantly improve my practical confidence.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
I improved both technical and soft skills during this period, including cloud deployment workflow, documentation discipline, team communication, and structured problem-solving. Mentorship and peer feedback gave me a clearer long-term career direction in cloud engineering.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The culture is respectful, proactive, and growth-oriented. Everyone contributes seriously while keeping communication open and friendly. Even as an intern, I was trusted with meaningful tasks and felt like a real member of the team.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
Internship policies were practical and intern-friendly. Flexible scheduling when needed, learning opportunities, and internal sharing sessions created a strong foundation for continuous improvement.

### Additional Questions
- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
	The most satisfying part was seeing my work become real deliverables and receiving constructive feedback that helped me improve quickly.

- **What do you think the company could improve for future interns?**  
	A more structured onboarding checklist for the first 1-2 weeks and a fixed monthly sharing session for intern projects would make the experience even better.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
	Yes, definitely. I would recommend this internship because interns receive real responsibilities, strong mentorship, and a supportive environment to grow.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations
- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
	Continue building project-based learning, add clearer milestone tracking, and maintain frequent mentor check-ins.

- **Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
	Yes. I would be very happy to continue and contribute to larger cloud projects in the next phase.

- **Any other comments (free sharing):**  
	Thank you to the FCAJ team for creating a meaningful internship journey. I am optimistic about applying what I learned and contributing more value in future projects.
